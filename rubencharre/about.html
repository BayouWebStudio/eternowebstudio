The Artist

About
Ruben Charre

Tattoos That Tell Your Story

I'm Ruben Charre — a tattoo artist from San Luis Potosí, Mexico with over 15 years in the craft. My style is rooted in neo-traditional illustration: bold linework, rich color, and the kind of detail that holds up for decades. But more than technique, what drives me is the story behind the piece. Anime characters that defined a generation. Portraits that carry grief and love in the same breath. Mythological imagery that speaks to who someone actually is.

I work out of Black Soul Tattoo SLP, where I've built a space that feels less like a studio and more like a creative home. I take on long-format projects — sleeves, back pieces, multi-session builds — as well as single-sitting work when the concept calls for it. If you're looking for someone to rush you through a flash sheet, I'm probably not your guy. But if you want to sit down, talk through something real, and walk out with something that actually means something? That's exactly what I'm here for.

Over the years I've had the honor of tattooing at the Mondial du Tatouage in Paris, traveling to conventions across Mexico and internationally, and connecting with artists and clients from all over the world. That exposure sharpens the work — every convention is a masterclass in seeing how other artists solve the same problems I do. But home is always San Luis Potosí. That's where the roots are.

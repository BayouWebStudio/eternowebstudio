About

Fifteen Years of Ink, Story, and Intention

I'm Ruben Charre, a tattoo artist born and based in San Luis Potosí, Mexico. My work lives at the intersection of neo-traditional illustration and the stories my clients carry — whether that's an anime character that shaped their childhood, a portrait of someone they love, or a piece of mythology they've carried in their chest for years. Every session is a conversation, and every tattoo is a chapter.

I've had the privilege of tattooing internationally — from the Mondial du Tatouage in Paris to conventions across Mexico and beyond — but home is always Black Soul Tattoo SLP. This is where I sharpen my craft, push my detail work, and build long-term projects with people who trust the process.
